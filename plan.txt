 — D&D App Outline —
Objectives
  Users will be able to...
    Create an account
    Login with username and password
    Create multiple characters
    Save progress of each character

  Player will be able to...
		Explore and act freely
		Interact with any NPC
		Equip items to weapon and armor slots

	NPC’s should...
		Act on a turn based system with player

	Locations should...
		Consist of continents, provinces, and areas
		Have distinct and descriptive travel routes
		Have hidden areas
		Be able to link together
